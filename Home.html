About Us

We love music

We are the Music Section of the cultural society of Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Tecgnology, MUTANTS. We do not make music, but bring out the notes present in everybody to make the rhythm that resonates with the soul. We are a harmony of voices and instruments that represent the transition from classical to modern forms of music with every note.

Mutants has two departments for music :

The Indian Department which consists of vocalists ranging from light vocals to pure classical styles of singing. The instruments include flute, tabla, morchand, mouth organ, guitar (acoustic and electric), ukulele and keyboards.

The Western Department which consists of singers having styles that include rap and falsetto along with a variety of instruments like guitars, ukulele, keyboards, congo, cajon and drums.
