Events and Acheivements

Anugoongj 2017

Both Indian and Western departments participated in the solo and group events for vocals and instruments in the annual cultural fest of IPU. Prakul Sharma(Western) was the runner up for the title Mr. Anugoonj.

Skill India Pre Final-I

The Indian Department of Mutants delivered a guest performance at the opening ceremony of the first Pre-Final India round(held at IIIT Delhi) for the World Skills Competition for Electronics held in Abu Dhabi.

Skill India Pre Final-II

The Indian Department performed at Maharaja Surajmal Insitute of Technology at the opening ceremony of the second Pre-Final India round for the World Skills Competition for Electronics.

Skill India Finals

The Indian Department gave a guest performance at Delhi Technological University at the opening ceremony of the India-Final round for the World Skills Competition for Electronics.

Anugoongj 2018

Mutants participated in Anugoonj 2018. The Indian Department won the prelims and were the second runner ups in the final round for Group Vocals.
